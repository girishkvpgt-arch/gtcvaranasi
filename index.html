A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a small, fixed sum of money regularly (e.g., monthly) into a mutual fund scheme rather than making a one-time lump sum payment. Over time, SIPs offer the benefits of rupee cost averaging and power compounding to build large financial reserves.
